Chiefs Comeback vs 49ers | 27 -24

The Daily Paper

The Kansas City Chiefs make an incredible comeback against the San Francisco 49ers. Down 24 - 10 in the 4th quarter of last night's Super Bowl, Patrick Mahomes and the Chiefs finally turned on the engines. Mahomes threw an absolute bomb to Tyreek Hill to cut the lead to 7 points. After a quick three-and-out by the 49ers, Mahomes and Hill connect again to knot things up at 24. Kevin Hill intercepts Tom Brady on the very next position to set up Taysum Jones for the go-ahead field goal.
